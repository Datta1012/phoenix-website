Access Control Systems

At Phoenix Technico, we provide smart and secure access control solutions that help organizations manage, monitor, and restrict entry to sensitive areas. Our systems are designed for offices, industries, schools, hospitals, banks, and residential complexes.

Our access control systems improve security, reduce unauthorized entry, and maintain digital records of every person entering or leaving your premises.

Key Features of Our Access Control Systems

  • Biometric (Fingerprint & Face) authentication
  • RFID / Smart card-based entry
  • PIN and password-based systems
  • Real-time access logs and reports
  • Remote monitoring and control
  • Integration with CCTV and attendance systems
  • Multi-door control support
  • Backup power support

Types of Access Control Solutions We Provide

1) Biometric Access Control

Uses fingerprint or facial recognition for highly secure entry. Ideal for offices and high-security areas.

2) RFID / Card-Based Access

Employees use smart cards or tags to gain entry. Commonly used in corporate offices and institutions.

3) Mobile-Based Access

Smartphone-based access using QR codes or Bluetooth for modern workplaces.

4) Door Lock Automation

Electronic door locks integrated with access systems for automated security.
