Marketing Executive

Location: Pune

Job Type:Full-Time Marketing Executive

CTC: ₹1.2L - 1.8L Annually

Job Description:

Phoenix Technico is looking for a dynamic and creative Marketing Executive to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a passion to Identify and approach potential companies for IT services.

Key Responsibilities:
    Client Acquisition (Business Development):
  • Identify and approach potential companies for IT services.
  • Visit client offices and present company services.
  • Generate new business opportunities. Build long-term relationships with corporate clients.
  • Create engaging posts, stories, and videos to increase visibility and engagement.
    • Corporate Marketing & Sales:
  • Meet decision-makers like IT Managers, Admin Heads, Directors, and HR.
  • Explain IT solutions such as CCTV, Networking, Access Control, Fire Systems, and IT Infrastructure.
  • Prepare and present proposals to companies.
  • Negotiate pricing and close deals.
    • Client Relationship Management:
  • Develop blog articles, case studies, and marketing content.
  • Maintain strong relationships with existing clients.
  • Regular follow-ups with clients.
  • Handle client queries and service coordination.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction.
    • Coordination with Technical Team:
  • Understand client requirements
  • Coordinate with engineers and technical staff.
  • Ensure smooth project execution.
    • Reporting & Performance:
  • Maintain daily/weekly client visit reports.
  • Track leads and conversions.
  • Prepare business reports for management.
Qualifications:
  • Minimum 12th pass.
  • Pursuing or holding a degree in Marketing preferred.
  • 1–3 years of relevant experience preferred.
  • Strong team player with creative mindset.
Additional Requirements:
  • Must have a two-wheeler with valid license.
  • Local candidates preferred.
  • Immediate joining or within 15 days preferred.
Benefits:
