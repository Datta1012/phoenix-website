Fire Detection Systems

At Phoenix Technico, we design, install, and maintain advanced fire detection and alarm systems to protect lives, property, and critical assets. Our solutions are built using industry-standard equipment, smart sensors, and automated monitoring technologies.

Our fire detection systems are suitable for offices, industries, hospitals, schools, hotels, warehouses, and residential complexes. We ensure fast response, real-time alerts, and seamless integration with existing safety infrastructure.

Key Features of Our Fire Detection Systems

  • Early smoke and heat detection using smart sensors
  • Automatic alarm activation and emergency alerts
  • Central monitoring panel with real-time status
  • Integration with building automation and security systems
  • Remote monitoring via mobile app or cloud dashboard
  • Battery backup to work during power failure
  • Regular maintenance and AMC support

Types of Fire Detection Solutions We Provide

1) Conventional Fire Alarm System

Ideal for small offices and residential buildings. Divides the building into zones and alerts the control panel when smoke or heat is detected.

2) Addressable Fire Alarm System

Best for large commercial buildings, malls, hospitals, and factories. Each detector has a unique address, allowing precise identification of fire location.

3) Wireless Fire Detection System

Suitable for heritage buildings or places where wiring is difficult. Uses wireless sensors and central monitoring.
